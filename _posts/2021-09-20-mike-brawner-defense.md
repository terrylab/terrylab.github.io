Congratulations to Mike Brawner, who successfully defended his dissertation on September 20th, 2021. Mike's thesis examined how context and scale mediate the impact of climate on a widespread avian predator. We are incredibly proud of everything Mike accomplished and look forward to seeing where his career takes him next!
