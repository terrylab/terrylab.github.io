bio: >
  Rebecca leads the Terry Lab at Oregon State University's Department of Integrative Biology.
  Her research integrates the perspectives, tools, and data archives of biology and geology to
  understand how terrestrial communities — primarily small mammals in western North America —
  have responded to climate and land-use change across modern, historical, and paleontological
  timescales. She is particularly interested in the reliability of fossil and subfossil records
  as ecological proxies and in the factors that make species and communities more or less
  vulnerable to environmental change.
